Ed Sheeran sings into a microphone and thousands of voices chime in to amplify the lyrics to his latest single. The English singer-songwriter has sold millions of singles. Sheeran is a gifted songwriter and has written for mainstream artists such as Taylor Swift and One Direction. Sheeran has been playing guitar since a young age, so naturally the guitar is used heavily in his music. His style is simple and classic yet fresh and relevant to the current generations and the ones that came before him. A repeat at weddings and coffee shops, Sheeran's music tends to pop up everywhere you go.
